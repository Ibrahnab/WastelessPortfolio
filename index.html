Background

Students are very busy and occasionally eat alone. This comes with the issue that they have to choose what to eat every day and it is often the case that they have left over ingredients that just expire in the fridge. Food and health are always the topics when it comes to the fundamental needs of people in everyday life. Itâ€™s difficult to get a balance between food storing and consumption. One scenario is that one bought some foods from groceries, kept them in the refrigerator, then forgot the existence of some of them. When they found them and realized afterwards, the food had already passed the expiration date and had to end up in the garbage eventually, which is not only unsustainable but also a waste of money.

We want to make it easier for people to use all of their food before it goes bad and to provide easy, delicious, quick and cheap recipes that they can plan ahead for the week to prevent food waste.

For this project we are going to focus on making a web application minimal viable product (MVP) that helps students plan their meals to prevent unnecessary food-waste. Further, the application will hopefully make it easier to plan what meals to cook for the week based on the available ingredients.
